who, what, why

About Us

Experienced Staff

We want to provide you with the best service which is why we have the best staff. All of our staff has been thouroughly trained. We did our best to get a friendly bunch of employees who love customer service, (almost as much as they love coffee). Come in and see how we can help you.

Cozy Locations

Both of our locations are located in downtown Boise within walking distance of Boise State Univeristy. Our location on Main Street is full of plants, all taken care of by our lovely employee Melissa. The location on 8th Street has a nice modern feel, with lots of seating. Both of our locations have specific purposes, each with a different set of people in mind. Come visit us and figure out what location suits you best.

Our Community

We believe that everyone should have a comfortable, inviting environment to work or hang around in. That is why we decided to open our own coffee shop, so that the people of the our community could have a place to meet people and feel comfortable at.
